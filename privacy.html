Privacy Policy

GEO CONSULTANTS is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Cyprus law.

Who We Are

References to "we" or "us" mean the company. "You" refers to the individual providing personal data.

When We Collect Personal Data

We may collect personal data in the following situations:

  • When you request legal advice or services.
  • When you contact us via the website or email.
  • When you provide goods or services to the company.
  • Through cookies on our website (unless disabled).

What Data We Collect

  • Contact and personal information (name, address, email, phone).
  • Payment information (bank account details, invoices).
  • Records of visits to our premises.

Why We Collect Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to requests for legal advice or other inquiries.
  • Provide legal, corporate, or administrative services.
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., anti-money laundering laws).
  • Send newsletters or other material only with your consent.

Sharing Your Data

We will not share your data except:

  • With third-party service providers (e.g., document management, IT).
  • With collaborators such as lawyers, banks, or accountants.
  • When required by law or for legal proceedings.
  • With your explicit consent.

Cookies

The website uses cookies to recognize your computer and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some website functions may not work properly. See our Cookies Policy for details.

Data Retention

  • Client data: retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 6 years.
  • Data from other collaborations: retained for the duration of the contract plus 6 years.

Security

We take all necessary measures to protect your data from unauthorized use or loss. Third-party processors acting on our behalf follow the same security standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete data when no longer necessary.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Transfer your data to another provider.
  • Withdraw consent at any time if processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights or for any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at info@geoconsultants.eu
