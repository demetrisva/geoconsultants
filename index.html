Why Cyprus

Business Hub

Strategic location, modern legal framework, double-tax treaties, and English-speaking professional ecosystem position Cyprus as a reliable European business hub.

Tax Advantages

  • Corporate tax 12.5% (rising to 15% in 2026, remaining competitive).
  • Dividend exemptions where >50% active income and ≥6.25% effective tax.
  • No withholding on intra-EU dividends; capital gains exemptions for shares/securities.

Infrastructure

High-quality professional services, fast incorporation timelines, modern banking and digital government services.

Quality of Life

Excellent infrastructure, favorable relocation programs, and a Mediterranean lifestyle attractive to entrepreneurs and families.

FAQ

How quickly can we incorporate a Cyprus company?

Do you provide registered office and secretarial?

Yes. We can provide registered office, secretarial, minutes, and annual filings with the Registrar.

Can you assist with banking and EMI onboarding?

We prepare documentation packages and guide you through the KYC/AML process with banks and EMIs.

Do you work with international shareholders?

Yes. We routinely support cross-border shareholders and directors and coordinate multi-jurisdictional documentation.
