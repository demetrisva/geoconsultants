Why Cyprus

Business Hub

Strategic location, modern legal framework, and a business‑friendly tax system place Cyprus among Europe’s reliable business hubs.

Tax Advantages

  • Corporate tax 12.5% rising to 15% in 2026, remaining competitive.
  • Dividends from subsidiaries tax‑exempt where >50% active income and ≥6.25% effective tax.
  • No withholding tax on intra‑EU dividends.
  • No capital gains tax on sale of shares or securities in subsidiaries.

Services & Infrastructure

High‑quality professional services, fast company registration, and modern banking, legal, and accounting infrastructure.

Quality of Life

Excellent infrastructure, favorable relocation programs, and a high standard of living for entrepreneurs and families.
