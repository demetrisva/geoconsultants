Why Cyprus

Why Choose Cyprus?

Cyprus has established itself as one of the most reliable business hubs in Europe, thanks to its strategic location, modern legal framework, and business-friendly tax system.

Tax Advantages:

  • The corporate tax rate is currently 12.5% and will increase to 15% in 2026 to align with international standards. Despite this increase, it remains competitive compared to other European countries.
  • Additionally, dividends received from subsidiaries are tax-exempt, provided the subsidiary generates more than 50% active income and is subject to an effective tax rate of at least 6.25%.
  • There is no withholding tax on dividends paid to shareholders within the EU, facilitating free movement of profits.
  • Finally, Cyprus does not impose capital gains tax on the sale of shares or securities in subsidiaries, offering flexible exit options.

Services & Infrastructure:

Cyprus offers high-quality professional services, a fast company registration process, and modern banking, legal, and accounting infrastructure.

Quality of Life:

With excellent infrastructure, favorable relocation programs, and a high standard of living, Cyprus is an ideal destination for entrepreneurs and their families.

What is the procedure for opening a bank account?

The full process in local banks may be completed within 30 days, while in EMIs can be completed within 2 weeks.

Can a non-resident open a bank account?

Yes, many banks accept non-residents, but the requirements are stricter (e.g. additional documentation, tax declaration from the country of residence).

What documents are required for opening of bank account?

For directors and UBOs: a valid ID or passport, proof of address, source of funds, source of wealth, and tax identification number. For companies: the certificate of incorporation, the company‚Äôs constitutional documents, and the company structure. Please note that banks may request additional documents at their discretion.

How long does it take to incorporate a company in Cyprus?

The incorporation process is generally completed within 10 to 13 working days, provided that the company name has been approved by the Registrar of Companies and all necessary documentation is duly submitted. An expedited procedure may also be arranged if required.

Can foreign individuals or entities establish a company in Cyprus?

Yes. Cypriot legislation allows 100% foreign ownership of private companies. Non-residents may act as shareholders, directors, or beneficial owners, subject to the completion of standard KYC and AML procedures.
