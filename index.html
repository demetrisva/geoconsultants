Why Choose Cyprus?

Cyprus is one of Europe’s most reliable business hubs with a strategic location, modern legal framework, and business-friendly tax systems.

  • Competitive corporate tax rates (12.5%, rising to 15% in 2026 with EU compliance)
  • Tax exemptions on dividends within the EU
  • No withholding tax on dividends paid to EU shareholders
  • No capital gains tax on securities sales
  • High-quality professional services and infrastructure
  • Excellent quality of life for entrepreneurs and families
