Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies

Our website uses cookies—small files stored on your computer—to improve your experience. This page explains what cookies we use, why, and how you can manage them.

How We Use Cookies

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for basic website functions, like submitting forms.

2. Functionality Cookies

Remember your choices (e.g., language) to improve your experience.

3. Analytical / Navigation Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use the site and improve it. These collect information anonymously, such as visitor numbers, pages viewed, and referral sources.

Disabling Cookies

You can change your browser settings to block cookies, but some website features may not work properly.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from trusted third parties, such as Google Analytics, to track site usage and improve content. These cookies do not personally identify you. Google Analytics may also be used for targeted advertising and measuring ad performance.

IP Addresses

We may use your IP address and browser type to analyze site usage and troubleshoot issues. Your IP address alone does not personally identify you.

Your Choice

By using this website, you agree to the use of cookies. You can disable them in your browser, but some site features may not work properly.
